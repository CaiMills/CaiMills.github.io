Deep Dungeons

This was made for one of my first year university modules, I made the game entirely by myself.

The gameplay consists of fighting against enemies whilst trying to escape the dungeon. To do this, the player has access to three different melee weapons, as well as dodge manouvere that can help avoid damage.

The features I worked on; 

• Movement Mechanics
• Level Design
• UI design     
• Melee Attack System
• Enemy AI https://github.com/CaiMills/Deep-Dungeons
• Dodge Roll
• Different Weapon Types
• Player Damage and Healing

Tools Used; 

• Unity
• Aseprite
