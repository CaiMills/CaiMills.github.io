About Deep Dungeons 
        
        Platform: PC, Windows
        Engine: Unity 2D
        Production Time: 5 Weeks
        Size of Team: 1

https://github.com/CaiMills/Deep-Dungeons

Overview

The game consists of engaging in combat with foes while attempting to escape the dungeon. To achieve this, the player can utilize three distinct melee arms and evasive actions that may aid in avoiding harm, though this comes at the expense of the player’s stamina meter, which gradually refills over time.
