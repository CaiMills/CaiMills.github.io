Deep Dungeons

 

https://github.com/CaiMills/Deep-Dungeons

History behind the Game;

This was made for one of my first year university modules, I made the game entirely by myself. The theme and gameplay behind the project were completely up to me, with the only rules being that the project must be 2D and made in Unity.

About the Game;

The gameplay consists of fighting against enemies whilst trying to escape the dungeon. To do this, the player has access to three different melee weapons, as well as dodge manouvere that can help avoid damage.

The features I worked on; 

• Movement Mechanics
• Level Design
• UI design     
• Melee Attack System
• Enemy AI 
• Dodge Roll
• Different Weapon Types
• Player Damage and Healing

Tools Used; 

• Unity
• Aseprite
