Slinging Slimes

This was my first ever game jam project, and was done in an introductory jam held by Staffordshire University. This game was created by me and a group of all fresh programmers and one artist, so I took the role as the head programmer as I had the most experience with Unity, although at the time it was still at a very amateur level.

The gameplay consists of collecting small slimes dotted throughout the level in order to grow in size, allowing you to reach greater heights with your jumps.

The features I worked on; 

• Movement Mechanics
• Baby Slime Mechanic
