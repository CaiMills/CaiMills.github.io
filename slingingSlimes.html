Slinging Slimes

 

https://cai-mills.itch.io/slinging-slimes https://github.com/CaiMills/Slinging-Slimes-Game-Jam History Behind the Game

This was my first ever game jam project, and was done in an introductory jam held by Staffordshire University. This game was created by me and a group of all fresh programmers and one artist, so I took the role as the head programmer as I had the most experience with Unity, although at the time I was still at a very amateur level. The game jams theme was 'Connect'.

About the Game

The gameplay consists of collecting small slimes dotted through-out the level in order to grow in size, connecting with them, allowing you to reach greater heights with your jumps.

The Features I Worked On 
    • Movement mechanics
    • Baby slime / size increase
    • Camera controls     
    • Level design       
    • Helped with UI design
    • Main menu
    • Audio
