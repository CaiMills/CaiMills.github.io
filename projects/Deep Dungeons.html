Deep Dungeons
PROJECT STATUS: Completed
PROJECT TYPE: Game
ENGINE: Unity
LANGUAGES: C#
PRODUCTION TIME: 8 weeks

Overview

'Deep Dungeons' is a Unity top-down 2D action game, where the goal is to navigate the different rooms of the dungeon to find the escape.

Features of this Game

Dodge Roll

A fully featured dodge roll that moves the player fowards and provides them with a couple frames of invinciblity, it consumes stamina and is also on a little timer so it can't be spammed by the player.

Weapon Switching

Once the player finds and interacts with another one of the weapons laid out throughout the dungeon, they will then equip it along with all its stats include damage and attack range, with new animations too.
