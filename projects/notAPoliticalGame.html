Overview

This was made for the 'Ukie Game Jam 2025', a 32 hour game jam. The game jams theme of choice was Contrast .The project was developed by me and 3 other programmers. Because of the games simplicity in terms of mechanics, I ended up being charge of the UI programming as well as the art, as our team lacked a dedicated artist. I also ended up supporting the other programmers in some of there parts due to their lack of experience using GDScript.

The gameplay revolves around answering Political questions given to you by the public, with each decision you make effecting one of two stats, total money spent and public moral, which is totalled up to show the general publics approval of you. Your goal is to end up with a higher approval rating than your opponent. Your opponent will always choose the Contrasting opinion to yours.
