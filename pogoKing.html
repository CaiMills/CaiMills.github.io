Pogo King

This was made for one of my first year university modules, it was my first time using a programming framework known as 'SDL', and so its quite rough around the edges, but because of it I know the basics of SDL, as well as how to port games to a Linux based platform, in this case being a system known as the 'Evercade'.

The gameplay consists of platforming to the end of each level, using a melee attack of which when it collides with certain objects in the environment will lead to different results, such as launching the player fowards or bouncing the player.

The features I worked on; 

• Movement Mechanics
• Melee Attack https://github.com/CaiMills/Pogo-King
• Scene Transitions
• Collisions
• Orb Mechanics (these are the objects in the environment that have unique properities
• Gravity and Jumping

Tools Used; 

• Visual Studio
• SDL
• Aseprite
