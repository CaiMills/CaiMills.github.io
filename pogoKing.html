Pogo King

 

https://github.com/CaiMills/Pogo-King

History behind the Game;

This was made for one of my first year university modules, it was my first time using the programming framework 'SDL' and so its quite rough around the edges, but because of it I now know the basics of SDL, as well as how to port games to a Linux based platform, in this case being a system known as the 'Evercade'.

About the Game;

The gameplay consists using the player movement to traverse the enviroment, more specifically using his melee attack. This attack, when colliding with certain objects in the environment known as orbs, will trigger unique effects that the player must utlise in order to navigate each level, these unique effects include an orb that bounces the player in the opposite direction the orb was hit, an orb that begins to move once hit, as well as an orb that dashes the player in the direction it was hit.

The features I worked on; 

• Movement Mechanics
• Melee Attack
• Scene Transitions
• Collisions
• Orb Mechanics (these are the objects in the environment that have unique properities)
• Gravity and Jumping
