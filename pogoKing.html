Pogo King

 

https://github.com/CaiMills/Pogo-King History Behind the Game

This was made for one of my first year university modules, I created this using the programming framework 'SDL' of which I have never used, which is why it's not entirely polished. I have acquired a basic understanding of SDL and how to transfer games to a Linux platform as a result of this project, in this case being for a system known as the 'Evercade'.

About the Game

The gameplay involves utilizing the player's unique movement techniques to move around the environment, specifically their melee attack. Unique effects will be generated for the player when this attack collides with orbs in the environment. These unique effects include an orb that bounces the player in the opposite direction the orb was hit, an orb that begins to move once hit, as well as an orb that dashes the player towards the direction it was hit.
